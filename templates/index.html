DIABETIC RETINOPATHY

Diabetic Retinopathy is a chronic eye disease caused due to diabetes. Almost one-third of the people who have diabetes are diagnosed with this. Early detection of this condition is critical for a good prognosis. On our platform you can detect your diabetic retinopathy grade in seconds. The model classifies the grade from 0-4. where

0 - No_DR
1 - Mild
2 - Moderate
3 - Severe
4 - Proliferate_DR
